6 Project Methodology & Tools

Methodology (Scrum)

The project follows the Scrum framework, which organizes work into short, focused sprints. Each sprint is guided by a prioritized backlog in Jira and tracked on a sprint board, ensuring transparency and accountability. By breaking down the work into small, manageable increments, the team can deliver value continuously while maintaining the flexibility to adapt to new requirements or insights. Regular sprint reviews and retrospectives support continuous improvement, helping refine both the deliverables and the process itself..

Tool Selection (Jira)

Jira is selected as the primary project management tool due to its robust support for Agile practices. It provides Scrum and Kanban boards, backlog management, and dependency tracking, making it well-suited for organizing complex work. The platform also encourages good project management habits, such as prioritization, progress tracking, and issue resolution, while aligning with industry standards. By using Jira, the team gains hands-on experience with a widely adopted professional tool, better preparing them for real-world project environments..
